# Human IQ Proposal Follow-Up Sequence

## 1. Submission Received
Subject:
`We received your Growth & Conversion Review`

Template:

Hi `{{first_name}}`,

Thank you for submitting your Growth & Conversion Review.

We are reviewing your intake now and will follow up with the clearest recommendation for your business. You do not need to book a call to keep the process moving. If we need clarification, we will reach out directly.

Best,  
Human IQ

## 2. Recommendation or Proposal Delivered
Subject:
`Your Human IQ recommendation`

Template:

Hi `{{first_name}}`,

We reviewed your submission and prepared the recommended next step for `{{business_name}}`.

The short version: `{{one_line_recommendation}}`

If you would like to move forward, reply with `approved` and we will send the invoice or deposit step along with onboarding details. If you would prefer to talk it through first, we can schedule a short call.

Best,  
Human IQ

## 3. Follow-Up Reminder
Subject:
`Checking in on your Human IQ recommendation`

Template:

Hi `{{first_name}}`,

Following up on the recommendation we sent for `{{business_name}}`.

If this is still a priority, reply here and we will move the next step forward. If timing has shifted, that is completely fine as well.

Best,  
Human IQ

## 4. Close-the-Loop
Subject:
`Closing the loop`

Template:

Hi `{{first_name}}`,

I wanted to close the loop on the recommendation we sent over.

If this is something you want to revisit later, just reply when the timing is right. If you would like us to reopen the proposal now, we can do that as well.

Best,
