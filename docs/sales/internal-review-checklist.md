# Human IQ Internal Review Checklist

## Purpose
Use this checklist to review a lead and approve the right recommendation in under 10 minutes.

## 10-minute review checklist
1. Confirm the submission has the basics: business name, contact, email, website, revenue range, urgency, and investment range.
2. Read the intake once for the business story, not just the score.
3. Confirm the primary problem diagnosis is accurate.
4. Confirm one primary engagement path has been selected.
5. Check that the recommendation matches the lead's maturity, urgency, and likely buying readiness.
6. Make sure the proposal does not give away a full implementation roadmap for free.
7. Check that the proposed scope is realistic for the path being recommended.
8. Check the pricing or offer band is directionally right for the path, if pricing is being included.
9. Confirm the next step is explicit and easy to act on asynchronously.
10. Confirm the recommendation can be delivered well if the client says yes.

## QA criteria
### Diagnosis accuracy
- Is the main revenue leak correctly identified?
- Does the recommendation match the actual problem, not just the loudest symptom?

### Correct engagement path
- Does the lead fit exactly one primary path?
- Would a different path create less friction or better protect scope?

### Scope realism
- Is the recommendation narrow enough to deliver well?
- Is anything being implied that Human IQ is not ready to operationalize?

### Pricing or offer band check
- Is the recommendation consistent with the intended level of engagement?
- Is the lead being over-sold or under-sold?

### Clear next step
- Can the client clearly understand how to proceed?
- Can they accept without needing a call first?

### Deliverability check
- If accepted today, could Human IQ onboard and begin cleanly?
- Are there any missing commercial or operational details?
