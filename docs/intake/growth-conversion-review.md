## 2. Internal Scoring Rubric

### A. Fit Score (1–5 each)

**1. Revenue / business maturity**

* 1 = very early stage, low structure
* 3 = operating business, some traction
* 5 = established business, clear commercial maturity

**2. Lead volume potential**

* 1 = very low volume / limited demand
* 3 = moderate volume with room to improve
* 5 = strong volume or clear upside opportunity

**3. Urgency**

* 1 = casually exploring
* 3 = problem acknowledged but not urgent
* 5 = immediate business priority

**4. Process readiness**

* 1 = little to no process
* 3 = partial process, inconsistent execution
* 5 = existing process that can be optimized quickly

**5. Budget readiness**

* 1 = little sign of budget capacity
* 3 = possible fit depending on scope
* 5 = clear willingness and ability to invest

**Fit score total:** /25

---

### B. Problem Classification

Choose one primary category and, if needed, one secondary category.

**1. Lead generation problem**
Not enough opportunities entering the pipeline.

**2. Qualification problem**
Too many poor-fit leads are reaching the sales process.

**3. Follow-up problem**
Slow responses, weak consistency, or too few touches.

**4. Sales process problem**
Leads exist, but conversion from conversation to close is weak.

**5. Systems problem**
Tools, ownership, tracking, and pipeline visibility are fragmented.

---

### C. Revenue Opportunity Score

**High opportunity**

* High lead volume but weak conversion
* Long response delays
* Poor qualification
* Inconsistent ownership
* Obvious leakage in the pipeline

**Medium opportunity**

* Moderate volume and moderate inefficiency
* Some process already exists
* Several visible improvement areas

**Low opportunity**

* Very low volume
* Low urgency
* Limited commercial readiness
* Problem not clearly defined

---

### D. Engagement Recommendation

**1. Not a fit yet**
Use when business readiness, urgency, or budget is too low.

**2. Strategy / audit only**
Use when the client needs clarity before implementation.

**3. Strategy + implementation sprint**
Use when there is clear opportunity and readiness to act.

**4. Ongoing optimization partner**
Use when the client already has demand and needs continued refinement across lead flow, qualification, and follow-up.

## 10. Recommended Typeform Logic

### Logic Goal

Use light logic only. Keep the experience fast and frictionless.

### Branching Rules

**Rule 1 — Enough leads**
If Question 15 = **No**, flag as likely lead-generation issue.

**Rule 2 — Unqualified leads**
If Question 18 = **Often** or **Sometimes**, flag as qualification issue.

**Rule 3 — Slow response**
If Question 21 = **1–2 days**, **3+ days**, or **It varies a lot**, flag as follow-up issue.

**Rule 4 — Weak follow-up consistency**
If Question 23 = **No**, flag as follow-up / sales-process issue.

**Rule 5 — Weak systems**
If Question 29 = **Disorganized** or **We do not really have a system**, flag as systems issue.

**Rule 6 — High urgency**
If Question 33 = **Immediate priority**, mark as higher-priority lead.

**Rule 7 — Buying intent**
If Question 34 = **Yes, immediately** or **Yes, soon**, mark as warmer sales lead.

**Rule 8 — Budget readiness**
If Question 35 = **$2,500–$5,000**, **$5,000–$10,000**, or **$10,000+**, mark as stronger commercial fit.
