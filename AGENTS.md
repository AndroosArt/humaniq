# Human IQ Agent Guide

## Purpose
+This repository supports the Human IQ launch.
+Primary front-door offer: Growth & Conversion Review.
+Primary downstream diagnostic/deliverable: Practical AI Blueprint.

## Working Style
+Optimize for speed to launch, clarity, and low operational complexity.
+Prefer simple, editable documents and lightweight implementation over frameworks or tooling overhead.
+Do not introduce enterprise processes, CI/CD, or automation unless it clearly reduces founder workload in the next 30 days.

## Source of Truth
+`README.md` explains the repo and how work is organized.
+`docs/current-sprint.md` is the active execution document.
+`docs/launch-checklist.md` is the launch-prep checklist.

## ChatGPT and Codex Roles
+Use ChatGPT as planner, strategist, and copy/offer thinking partner.
+Use Codex as executor for file creation, editing, repo hygiene, implementation, and structured updates.
+When a request is ambiguous, prefer asking for one concrete decision rather than broad discovery.

## Guardrails
+Keep the offer language consistent with Human IQ positioning.
+Use "Growth & Conversion Review" for the front-door offer.
+Use "Practical AI Blueprint" for the downstream diagnostic/deliverable.
+Preserve founder-readable documentation. Avoid jargon when a plain-English phrase works.
+Do not silently rename files, move folders, or add dependencies without stating why.

## Repo Priorities
+Landing page and offer assets.
+Intake and qualification materials.
+Launch checklist and current sprint tracking.
+Only add app or automation code when it directly supports launch execution.

## Definition of Useful
+A change is useful if it helps publish, improve, or operationalize the offer.
+Default to the smallest change that improves launch readiness.
